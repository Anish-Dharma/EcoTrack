About Carbon Emission

What is Carbon Emission?

Carbon emissions refer to the release of carbon dioxide (CO‚ÇÇ) into the atmosphere, primarily from the burning of fossil fuels like coal, oil, and gas. These emissions contribute to climate change and global warming.

Major Means of Carbon Emission

  • Transportation (cars, buses, trains, airplanes)
  • Electricity and heat generation (especially from coal and gas)
  • Industrial activities (manufacturing, cement, steel)
  • Deforestation and land use changes
  • Agricultural practices and livestock

How to Reduce Carbon Emissions

  • Use public transportation, cycle, or walk
  • Switch to renewable energy sources like solar or wind
  • Reduce, reuse, and recycle materials
  • Improve home insulation and energy efficiency
  • Plant trees and support forest conservation
  • Adopt energy-efficient appliances
  • Reduce meat consumption and food waste
